Privacy Policy

Effective June 22, 2026

This policy describes how Gardens East handles information used by the Gardens East QuickBooks Note Assistant (the “App”). The App is a private, internal business tool and is not offered to the general public.

Information the App accesses

With an authorized user’s permission, the App may read QuickBooks Online company information, customers, products and services, payment terms, invoices, invoice line items, and payments. It also uses invoice-draft information supplied by Gardens East personnel.

How information is used

The information is used only to create a local reference snapshot, understand Gardens East’s historical invoice patterns, and prepare invoice drafts for human review. The App does not automatically create, modify, send, or post QuickBooks invoices.

Storage and retention

QuickBooks data and authorization credentials are stored locally on computers controlled by Gardens East. Information is retained only while it is useful for the App’s business purpose and may be deleted from those computers when it is no longer needed.

Sharing and sale of information

Gardens East does not sell personal information, use it for advertising, or share it with unrelated third parties. Information may be disclosed when required by law or when necessary to protect Gardens East’s legal rights.

Security

Gardens East uses reasonable administrative and technical safeguards intended to limit access to authorized personnel. No storage or transmission method can be guaranteed completely secure.

Your choices

An authorized QuickBooks administrator may revoke the App’s access through Intuit’s connected-app controls. Requests concerning locally stored information may be directed to the contact below.

Children

The App is a business tool and is not directed to children under 13.

Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated as the App changes. The effective date above will be revised when material changes are made.

Contact

Questions or requests concerning this policy may be sent to gerryldemers@gmail.com.
